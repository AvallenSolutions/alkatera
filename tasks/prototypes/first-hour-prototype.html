How alkatera works

This is a house, not a dashboard.

No menus three levels deep. Everything you do lives in one of seven rooms, and every room keeps its colour, so you always know where you are. The desk is the hall: you can see into every room from there, and Rosa can fetch anything from any of them. Let's take the walk.

The cellar
What you make: products, recipes, packaging and each one's footprint.
The workbench
What you measure: your sites, energy, water, waste and spend.
The evidence
What you can prove: reports, certifications and targets.
The network
Who you talk to: suppliers, messages and expert help.
The library
What we know: plain-language guides for your industry.
Today
The day ahead: Rosa's brief of what needs you, and what it's worth.
The wiring
Behind the walls: settings, billing and your team.
